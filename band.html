Imagine Dragons

Imagine Dragons is an American pop rock band from Las Vegas, Nevada, consisting of lead singer Dan Reynolds, lead guitarist Wayne Sermon, bassist Ben McKee, and drummer Daniel Platzman.They have won three American Music Awards, nine Billboard Music Awards, one Grammy Award, one MTV Video Music Award and one World Music Award.

Imagine Dragons' musical style has mainly been described as alternative rock, pop rock, indie rock, electronic rock, pop, and arena rock. Their music also has some influences of folk, R&B, hip hop and EDM.
